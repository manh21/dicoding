About

Gacha is a PWA Web built to show Genshin Impact Character Stats. That aim to make it easily available to everyone in desktop, laptop, tablet, and smartphone.

Genshin Impact is an open-world, action RPG developed and published by miHoYo for PC, iOS/Android, and PS4 platforms. The game is Free-to-Play with a Gacha monetization system in the form of Wishes.


Character

How to Obtain : Characters can primarily be obtained by using Primogems to perform Wishes (gacha pulls). The starter characters: Amber, Kaeya, and Lisa, can be obtained early on in the main storyline (Archon Quests). Some characters may also be purchased from the monthly Paimon's Bargains shop in exchange for Masterless Starglitter.

Leveling : Characters can gain experience primarily by completing quests and using Character EXP Materials. Characters will also get a very small amount of experience for defeating monsters.

Ascensions : Characters can be ascended to their next Phase once they reach their current max level up to 6 times (Lv.90). Upon reaching the 1st and 3rd Phase/ascension, the character unlocks a new passive talent. The 3rd passive talent, if the character has one, is already unlocked at Phase 0.
